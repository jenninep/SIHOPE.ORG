WHAT IS SIHOPE.ORG?

STATEN ISLANDERS NOW HAVE A PLACE TO TURN

As Staten Island continues to face the tragic drug epidemic plaguing our communities, too often we heard that when they needed help, Staten Islanders did not know where to turn. Despite the life-saving work being done by so many, it seemed that most Islanders did not know where to access these services.
Developed by District Attorney Michael E. McMahon, SIHOPE.org is a one-stop-shop for those battling addiction, those with a loved one who is struggling, or those who simply want to get involved in helping end this terrible crisis.
